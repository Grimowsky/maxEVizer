Tournament poker is bound with an inevitable scenario of a downswing that a backed player will not withstand - either financially or mentally. It’s not about the player that much, it’s about a magnitude. Everybody has their limits.

after applying static AGS

If we assume that the staking’s ‘stop loss’ is constant, the one way to increase backer’s end profit would be to increase player’s winnings. This time the make-up to split profits ratio will be better.

In cash games the quickest way to increase one’s profitability is to pick the games with weaker players - table selection. This simple rule is considered non transferable to tournaments, due to hundreds of different games running every day, differentiated by a number of both known and unpredictable variables and a vague relation between the results of their analysis and final profitability.
